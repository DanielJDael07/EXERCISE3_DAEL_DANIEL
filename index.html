Welcome to ILOILO CITY

Travel to Iloilo City and explore its many historical, cultural landmarks, and natural wonders. Find the top attractions that you should add in your travel itinerary. When you look at the map, you’ll see that this beautiful province is located in the middle of the Philippines. Because of its location and rich cultural heritage, Iloilo is given the nickname The Heart Of The Philippines and is one of the top Visayas tourist spots.
